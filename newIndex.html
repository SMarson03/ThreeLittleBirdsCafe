About Us

Jah Donovan grew up in the countryside of Jamaica with his grandmother who was primarily a coffee farmer. He learned to appreciate his time helping nourish the soil and enjoying the fruits of his labor. Living stateside he always missed the taste of the teas and coffees he grew up with. Three Little Birds is an homage to his grandmother. Sourcing all our coffee and teas from the Blue Mountain farmers in Jamaica. We make sure to stay an ethical and fair trade company. Taking pride ourself and the rich heritage of those before us.
